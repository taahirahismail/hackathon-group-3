What the people want to know:

How is my credit score calculated?

Credit scores are typically calculated using various factors, including payment history, credit utilization, length of credit history, types of credit used, and recent credit inquiries.

What is a good credit score range?

Credit scores typically range from 300 to 850. Generally, a score above 700 is considered good, while scores above 800 are considered excellent.

1203

How often is my credit score updated?

Credit scores are updated regularly, usually on a monthly basis. However, the exact frequency may vary depending on the credit reporting agency and the specific scoring model used.

349

What factors can negatively impact my credit score?

Factors such as late payments, high credit card balances, defaulting on loans, and having too many recent credit inquiries can negatively impact your credit score.

4985

How long does negative information stay on my credit report?

Most negative information, such as late payments or accounts in collections, can stay on your credit report for up to seven years. Bankruptcies can remain on your report for up to 10 years.

4309

How can I improve my credit score?

You can improve your credit score by making timely payments, keeping credit card balances low, avoiding new credit applications, and maintaining a diverse credit mix.

Can I dispute errors on my credit report?

Yes, if you find any errors on your credit report, you have the right to dispute them with the credit reporting agencies. They are obligated to investigate and correct any inaccuracies.

239

How long does it take to see improvements in my credit score?

The time it takes to see improvements in your credit score depends on various factors, including the actions you take and the specific circumstances. Generally, it can take several months to see noticeable changes.

Does checking my credit score frequently affect my credit?

No, checking your credit score through the app or other legitimate methods does not typically impact your credit score. These are considered soft inquiries and do not leave a negative impact.

34590

Can I get a loan with a low credit score?

It may be more challenging to obtain a loan with a low credit score, but it is not impossible. Some lenders specialize in providing loans to individuals with lower credit scores, although they may come with higher interest rates or stricter terms.
